### Professional Profile
I am a **Robotics and AI Engineer** driven by the goal of using technology to make the world a little better. I specialize in bridging the gap between hardware and digital code, with a core focus on building reliable and scalable software for autonomous systems. 

Currently, I am a Master's student at **Syddansk Universitet (SDU)** in Odense, Denmark, specializing in Advanced Robotics Technology. My current research for my Master's Thesis focuses on **Uncertainty Estimation in Deep Neural Networks**. Specifically, I am using **Evidential Deep Learning** in PyTorch to explore how robots can be made safer and more reliable when utilizing AI for decision-making.

### Research & Work Experience
My background includes hands-on experience in both industrial and research environments:
* **SDU (HospiBot Project):** As a Robot Programmer, I worked on robotic mobile platforms for hospitals, focusing on PID controller reliability and developing image-based recognition pipelines for human-robot interaction.
* **Talgo:** Developed a Python-based testing tool from scratch to monitor train system responses using UDP and TCP sockets.
* **Siemens:** Gained experience in CNC programming and mechanical design using SINUMERIK and Siemens NX.
